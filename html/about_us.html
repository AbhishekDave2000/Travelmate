Our History

In 2021, a group of Indian-based young individuals had a dream but lacked financial resources. A dream to travel as much as possible and create experiences that would last a lifetime. With a dash of boldness, a deposit on a twelve-seater minibus, and an advertisement on a noticeboard, they'd discovered the solution. The very first trip was created.That summer went down in history, and the stories became legends almost immediately. So, when the spring of 2021 rolled along, they marketed the trip again. TravelMate Travel Limited was established in the Canada in 2022 after the founders realized they had discovered something unique.
